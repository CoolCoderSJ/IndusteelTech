Our Mission

Our mission is to empower underrepresented areas in cities by raising funds and investing in technology-driven solutions that foster their advancement. We envision a future where our events and hackathons serve as catalysts for positive change, giving passionate individuals the opportunity to address the challenges faced by cities using STEM-based solutions.
